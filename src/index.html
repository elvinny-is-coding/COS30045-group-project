Impact of Nutrition, Obesity, and Diabetes Mortality in the USA

Explore the visualized data on how nutrition, obesity, and diabetes affect health outcomes across the United States.

Overview

This project offers insights into the relationship between nutrition, obesity, and diabetes mortality in the USA. Through a series of interactive maps and charts, you can explore data on population distributions, obesity prevalence, and diabetes mortality rates to understand the impact of these factors on public health. Each section includes visualizations based on data analysis, providing a deeper look at geographical patterns and trends over time.
