Population of United States of America

Population Map Overview

This interactive population map is a key reference for understanding the geographical distribution of the population across the USA. It provides detailed insights into population densities, allowing users to visualize and analyze demographic patterns.

This map serves as a foundational tool in our project, which explores the relationship between nutrition, obesity, and diabetes mortality. By examining the population data, users can identify regions with higher or lower population concentrations, which can be critical when correlating obesity prevalence and diabetes mortality rates.

This population map, combined with other interactive charts and maps in the project, enables a comprehensive analysis of public health impacts, offering a clear view of how demographic factors intersect with health outcomes over time.
